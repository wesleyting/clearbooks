Frequently asked questions

What is your process?

Our process can be summed in four steps. We discuss your business to learn more about it, then strategize and develop a plan that best suits you. From there we bring you and your team onboard to our system, and finally we assist and support your business in the background and with anything that may arise.

View our About Page to learn more about our process.

When Should I do my Taxes?

It's always to be on top of your financials, which is why with us, all of your transactions and financial activity will be securely recorded and ready for the tax -filing deadline. We also help you catch up on your bookkeeping if they're out of date and in need of guidance to get back on the right track.

Who will be handle my books?

Our team of experts will handle your books, including our founder Pav who will overlook all clients and business conducted. Your books will always be in good hands, as our Cloud-Based accounting software will safely store all of your data, along with any previous copies and edits.

Why do I need to outsource my books?

For most businesses, taxes are a pain to handle, and something they often try to put off. This often becomes an obstacle to face down the road, and a major distraction for your business. We want to take care of that problem for you and have you focus on the big picture, while having a financial expert ready to assist you with any decision.
